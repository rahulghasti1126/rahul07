Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best fertilizer for my crops?

The best fertilizer depends on your soil type and crop requirements. We recommend getting your soil tested first. Generally, NPK fertilizers work well for most crops, but our experts can help you choose the perfect blend for your specific needs.

How often should I apply fertilizer?

Fertilizer application frequency depends on crop type and growth stage. For most crops, we recommend application every 4-6 weeks during the growing season. Our agriculture solutions team can create a customized fertilization schedule for your farm.

Are organic fertilizers better than chemical fertilizers?

Both have their advantages. Organic fertilizers improve soil health over time, while chemical fertilizers provide immediate nutrients. Many farmers find success with a combination approach. We offer both types and can advise on the best solution for your farm.

How do I store fertilizers properly?

Store fertilizers in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Keep them in their original containers with lids tightly closed. Avoid storing near food products or animal feed. Our staff can provide specific storage recommendations for each product.

What safety precautions should I take when using fertilizers?

Always wear gloves and protective clothing when handling fertilizers. Avoid inhalation of dust or fumes. Wash hands thoroughly after use. Store out of reach of children and animals. Follow all label instructions carefully.

How can I determine the right fertilizer dosage?

The right dosage depends on soil conditions, crop type, and growth stage. We recommend soil testing for accurate recommendations. Our experts can analyze your soil and provide precise dosage guidelines for optimal results.
